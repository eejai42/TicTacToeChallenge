An implementation of 3x3 Tic Tac Toe is expected to support a number of different kinds of players. Human players, learning players and Rules based Players. Currently, there are 5 player types in this app.

  1. Human
    No strategies are employed. Player of this type wil wait to be told what to do. A Human TicTacToe Interface (UI) is needed for this type of player.
    Human

  2. Beginner
    Wins, prevents a win or plays randomly.
    SimpleRules

  3. Intermediate
    Looks 1 move ahead and also follows all beginner rules.
    SimpleRules

  4. Advanced
    The best - all strategies are employed. This AI should never lose. It will only win if the opponent makes a mistake, but otherwise, it at least tie.
    SimpleRules

  5. AI
    An AI which has not pre-programmed rules, and instead just keeps track of his moves, and weights the moves that lead to a win more heavily that moves which don't.
    MLAI
